Fin Rot Treatment

Comprehensive guide to identifying and curing Fin Rot.

Fin rot is a bacterial or fungal infection often triggered by stress or poor water quality.

  • Frayed or ragged fins (torn edges).
  • Discoloration (white, black, or red edges).
  • Inflammation at the fin base.
  • Less active behavior or hiding.

Move the fish to a quarantine tank to prevent spreading the disease. Use a sponge filter and heater.

Ensure Ammonia and Nitrite are 0 ppm. Keep Nitrate low (< 40 ppm). Perform daily water changes.
