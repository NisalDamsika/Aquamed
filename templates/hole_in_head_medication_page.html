Hole in the Head Treatment

Guide to curing Hexamita and nutritional deficiencies.

Caused by Hexamita parasites or vitamin deficiency.

  • Pits or holes on the head/lateral line.
  • Stringy white feces.
  • Loss of appetite and weight loss.

Isolate the fish in a hospital tank to prevent spreading parasites and to monitor eating habits closely.

Remove activated carbon (it absorbs meds). Ensure 0 Ammonia/Nitrite.
