White Spot (Ich) Treatment

Comprehensive guide to identifying and curing White Spot disease.

White Spot (Ich) causes small white cysts on the body, fins, and gills.

  • White spots looking like salt grains.
  • Rubbing against objects (Flashing).
  • Clamped fins or gasping at surface.

Move the infected fish to a hospital tank. This prevents the parasite from spreading to other fish/substrate.

Slowly raise temp to 28–30°C (82–86°F) for 10–14 days. This speeds up the parasite's life cycle.
